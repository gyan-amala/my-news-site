**Gyan Amala respects your privacy. We believe in providing a secure, non-invasive reading experience.**

Because Gyan Amala is built as a lightning-fast static website without a traditional backend database, our data collection practices are minimal by design. 

### No Creepy Tracking
We do not aggressively track our users, run invasive background scripts, or sell personal data to third-party advertisers. You can browse our news feed and UPSC study hubs freely and anonymously.

### Contact Form Data
When you submit an article pitch, correction, or general feedback via our Contact Page, the information you provide (such as your name and email address) is securely routed through our form provider (Tally) and stored in a private Google Workspace database (Google Sheets). 

This data is used **solely** for the purpose of reviewing your submission and replying to your inquiry. We will never add you to a mailing list without your explicit consent.

### Third-Party Hosting
Gyan Amala is hosted securely on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect basic, anonymized web traffic data (such as server routing and edge node performance) to ensure the site remains fast, secure, and protected from malicious bot traffic.
