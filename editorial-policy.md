**At Gyan Amala, our editorial philosophy is anchored in truth, neutrality, and academic rigor.**

Because our readers rely on us for accurate global news and competitive UPSC examination preparation, we strictly adhere to the following editorial standards.

### Accuracy and Fact-Checking
All content published on Gyan Amala—from daily news briefs to comprehensive PSIR study notes—undergoes a thorough review process. We cross-reference historical data, quotes, and contemporary events against reliable, authoritative primary and secondary sources before publication.

### Non-Partisanship and Objectivity
Our political science and news content remains analytically neutral. We do not endorse political parties or ideological factions. We present multiple perspectives and theoretical frameworks, ensuring our analysis remains objective—mirroring the exact standard expected of a top-tier UPSC candidate.

### Corrections and Transparency
We are committed to accountability. If an error in reporting or academic documentation occurs, we will correct it promptly and transparently. 

If you spot a factual inaccuracy in our notes or news feed, we encourage you to reach out via our [Contact Page](/contact/) so our editorial desk can investigate and update the record.
