Following a tense two-day policy summit, the Central Bank board announced it will hold its benchmark interest rate steady, breaking a consecutive streak of incremental adjustments.

The decision was widely anticipated by domestic financial institutions as inflation indexes show signs of plateaus. Market indices reacted with modest gains immediately following the afternoon press conference.
